Are Online Casinos Legal in Florida? (Short Answer)

❌ No — online casinos are NOT legal in Florida.

Florida does not allow real-money online casino gambling, including online slots, table games, or online poker. There is no state-licensed online casino system in Florida.

Any website claiming to offer real-money online casino gambling legally in Florida is not regulated by the state.

Florida Gambling Laws: How They Work

Florida gambling laws are based on a combination of:

  • Florida state statutes

  • Voter-approved constitutional amendments

  • Tribal gaming agreements

Unlike states such as Pennsylvania or New Jersey, Florida has not passed legislation to legalize online casino gambling.

This is why the question are online casinos legal in Florida has a clear legal answer: they are not permitted under current Florida law.

What Types of Gambling Are Legal in Florida?

Although online casinos are illegal, Florida does allow limited forms of gambling.

Legal Gambling in Florida Includes:

  • State lottery

  • Pari-mutuel wagering (horse racing, dog racing where applicable, jai alai)

  • Tribal casinos operated by the Seminole Tribe

Florida’s tribal casinos operate under a gaming compact, which gives the Seminole Tribe exclusive rights to certain casino-style games.

Why Online Casinos Are Illegal in Florida

Florida voters approved Amendment 3, which requires voter approval for any expansion of casino gambling.

Because of this amendment:

  • The Florida legislature cannot legalize online casinos on its own

  • Any legalization would require a statewide vote

  • No such vote has approved online casinos

As a result, online casinos remain illegal in Florida.

Is Online Poker Legal in Florida?

❌ No — online poker is not legal in Florida.

Florida does not license or regulate online poker rooms. Any online poker site offering real-money play to Florida residents operates outside Florida regulation.

What About Sports Betting in Florida?

Sports betting in Florida exists under a tribal-only framework.

  • Sports betting is controlled by the Seminole Tribe

  • It operates through tribal agreements

  • It does not extend to full online casino gaming

Sports betting availability has faced legal challenges, reinforcing how strict Florida’s gambling laws are.

Are Social Casinos Legal in Florida?

Yes social casinos are legal, but they do not offer real-money gambling.

Social casinos:

  • Use virtual coins

  • Do not allow cash withdrawals

  • Operate under sweepstakes or entertainment models

These platforms are not online casinos in the legal sense and should not be confused with real-money gambling sites.

Are Offshore Online Casinos Legal in Florida?

❌ No. Offshore online casinos are not legal in Florida.

While many offshore websites accept players from Florida, they:

  • Are not licensed by the state

  • Offer no consumer protection

  • Operate outside U.S. jurisdiction

Florida law does not recognize these platforms as legal gambling operators.
